About LinguaLab

LinguaLab is a school with modern methods: an emphasis on conversational practice, and an individual approach. Our teachers are certified specialists with teaching experience in the country and abroad.

We use game-based approaches, role-playing exercises, and lots of talking from the first lesson.

Quick level test

Find out your starting level in 10 minutes - we will select a course for you.

Start test

Classes are conducted in small groups (up to 6 people) to ensure individual attention and maximum speaking practice. We use a communicative approach, focusing on real-life language use.

Our groups typically consist of 4-8 students, which allows for dynamic interaction and personalized feedback from the instructor.

Our Mission

At LinguaLab, our mission is to empower individuals to confidently communicate across cultures. We believe language learning should be an engaging, effective, and enjoyable journey for everyone.
