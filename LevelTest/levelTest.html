Level test

Choose a language to start the test. The questions gradually become more difficult. Don't guess - if you don't know the answer, skip the question.

1. ___ are you from?

2. She ___ TV every evening.

3. Did you ___ the new film last night?

4. I haven't finished my homework ___.

5. You ___ smoke in here. It's forbidden.

6. If I had known you were coming, I ___ a cake.

7. He can't ___ living in such a noisy city.

8. By the time the police arrived, the thief ___.

9. Not only ___ sing, but she also plays the piano.

10. I wish I ___ more time to travel.

Test key (English):

  • 1. b) Where
  • 2. b) watches
  • 3. a) see
  • 4. c) yet
  • 5. b) mustn't
  • 6. c) would have baked
  • 7. b) get used to
  • 8. a) had disappeared
  • 9. b) can she
  • 10. b) had
